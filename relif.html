Challenge

Unlike printed material, web content must be designed without knowing the size or orientation of the screen on which the content will be consumed. Laptops, tablets, phones, and watches are in use for a specific end-user based context and other factors. With responsive web design, designers make decisions about how content will be automatically hidden, resized, or re-arranged on these different devices.

Outcome

After performing secondary and guerrilla research, our team developed personas for three stakeholders within the domain, noting that not all stakeholders are necessarily end users of the responsive website:

  a. Customer

  b. Service provider

  c. Business owner

Responsive Design

This is known as responsive web design (RWD), where a single code base is adapted to display content optimally across devices with varying physical sizes, resolution, and capabilities.

Relif is a mobile app and online platform that provides disaster victims in the community to get connected to psychological practitioners anytime, anywhere.
